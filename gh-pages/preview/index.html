Flycast Dojo 7 Preview 2

Download ( Windows, Linux, Mac)

Flycast Dojo 7 is a complete rewrite on top of Flycast 2.3.2 to maintain compatibility with existing netplay savestates. You should be able to open the emulator and immediately join "Quick Match" for your game of choice.

You will be met with an Onboarding screen where you can set your player name, optionally specify an avatar, and select the Relay closest to you for fallback when hole punching doesn't work.

Bug reports are appreciated on on the Dojo Project Discord. Note that this is NOT a drop-in replacement for use in Fightcade just yet. This is being put up as a preview to test the functionality. I will make sure it has Fightcade compatibility on final release.
