Add to Steam Deck Library

With the latest Fightcade update, it's easier than ever to run Flycast Dojo natively on your Steam Deck.
  1. Enter Desktop Mode by holding the power button and selecting Switch to Desktop.
  2. Download and extract the Linux version of Fightcade into your Home directory
    (Fightcade destination folder will be /home/deck/Fightcade)
  3. Download the Flycast Dojo icon entry and place it your Desktop.
  4. Double-click on the icon and press Continue to authorize the shortcut. Close the Flycast Dojo window.
  5. Right click on the icon and press Add to Steam.

Flycast Dojo will be added to your Steam library with native controls. Any compatible games in Fightcade will show up in the emulator's main menu. You will also be able to access Flycast Dojo's Training Mode, Match Codes, and Relays without switching to Desktop Mode.
